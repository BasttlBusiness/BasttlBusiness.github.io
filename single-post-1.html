3 Most Common Car Dealer Traps to Avoid. Be Aware!

LBuying a car is a dream of everyone. If it would be the first car, then the excitement level is top of the roof. But mostly dealers take the advantage when you’re too desperate to buy a car. How can the dealer make you fool?

Don’t rush too much

This is the most common mistake. The reason is; we are too desperate to buy a car. But dealer take the disadvantage of your excitement.Instead of take time. Do your proper homework or research work before visiting showroom. And gathering as much information as you can.

Comparison is the key

Comparison is the best method of evaluation. You can buy a best car at a best price by comparison.So don’t only listen about one car brand, instead ask about different cars and compare their engine, price, servicing, mileage etc.

Buying accessories from the dealer

Accessories cover the major part of profit for the dealer or showroom. So they will try to sell you various accessories. And will scared you, if you talked about buying accessories somewhere else. So be aware! All these are traps. Not all the accessories, but few like seat covers, parking sensors, stereo system etc. you can easily buy somewhere else in very reasonable price.

Bonus Tip: Saying ‘No’ can save you

You can simply say no to the dealer and don’t be think too much. Ask questions and know more about car and don’t forget to take test drive.
