Car Wax Vs Car Polish

All of has a common wish, we all want to keep the shine of our car forever. No doubt, first impression of car decide by its shining. But by only car washing, it is not possible to always keep your car shiny. So every car needs car wax or car polish or sometimes both. Most of us don’t know the difference between car wax and car polish.

Car Polish

Surface scratches, swirls, oxidation, filth, and other minor flaws can all be removed using car polish. Car polish should be applied before wax. This is accomplished by removing a very thin layer of paint from the surface, reducing the visibility of scratches when they settle into the coat. Car polish find in cream and liquid both forms. It is applied gently and in a circular pattern. Before applying car polish, the old and fine layer of paint have been removed. Polishing is non toxic. So you can apply car polish by yourself and also with the help of professionals.The after effect of car polish is longer than the car wax. It can keep your car shiny up to one year.

Car Wax

If you want a shiny look with glossy touch then you should try car wax. Car wax protects and enhances the paint finish of a vehicle. To make your car’s paintwork shine, apply wax after cleaning and polishing. Carnauba wax is the most prevalent ingredient in vehicle wax. This particular component is fantastic in auto wax. Nowadays, some waxes are also coming up with UV inhibitors. That will help to prevent your car's paint from fading caused by the sun's rays. Car wax gives a perfect and glossy finish. Car wax gives your car shiny appearance with a smooth touch.
