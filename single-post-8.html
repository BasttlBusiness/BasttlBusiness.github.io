5 Ways to Improving Your Car’s Mileage

Mileage is the most essential thing that come into our mind before buying a new vehicle. While using a car, we all have a concern about mileage and be concerned why not, the mileage will directly affect our pocket or monthly budget.

#1 Appropriate tyre pressure

An appropriate tyre pressure will create a huge difference in the efficiency of car. Minimal pressure in tyre will increase the friction between road and the rubber. So due to this friction the fuel consumption will increase.

#2 Avoiding AC will improve 30% mileage

If you want to see the massive difference in the mileage then the best option is to turn off the air conditioner. This simple act will increase the mileage of your car up to 30%.

#3 More smoothly use = Less fuel consumption

Use the clutch, accelerator and break gently. If you suddenly accelerate your car and apply sudden break, then it will completely disturb the mileage of your car.

#4 Always be a route planer before starting journey

Do not get take a rough and congested road in the pursuit of short cut or saving fuel of your car. Such road will lead to overuse of clutch and that will reduce the mileage.

#5 Change gears smoothly and wisely

Gear changing is another most important factor. Always drive your car at appropriate gear and change the gear smoothly. This is valid for both the car whether it is manual or automatic.
