5 best used cars to buy under 5 lacs.

LAs more automobiles are introduced each month, the market is continuously growing. We've compiled a list of the greatest second-hand automobiles to buy in India. These hand-picked cars made the cut based on factors such as dependability, maintenance costs, passenger comfort, and brand value.

Maruti Suzuki Swift VDi

At a reasonable maintenance cost for a long period, VDi makes it first on the list.Among its many prominent feature, one that appeals a lot of buyers is the very wide range of colours VDi offers.

Hyundai i20

Hyundai has been producing the i20 supermini hatchback since 2008. India is currently a very large market for the i20 series. You sure don't want to miss out!

Renault DUSTER

Known for its superior SUV capabilities, stylish design and advance safety features, the Duster is a dream car for so many different families in India.

Ford Figo

It's availablity in both diesel and petrol version along with speed manual and power steering makes this one of the best choices of Cars under an affordable range.

Chevrolet Cruze

The touchscreen, which runs Chevrolet's simple MyLink infotainment menu, and the hard-button controls operate with a well-damped smoothness, make the surroundings even better than it appears.
